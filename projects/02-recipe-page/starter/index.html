Vegetarian recipe

Creamy Tomato Pasta

A cozy pasta dinner with a smooth tomato sauce, fresh basil, and simple pantry ingredients.

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

Total time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Dietary label: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces dried pasta, such as penne or fusilli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes, 15 ounces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or unsweetened oat cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt, plus more for the pasta water
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated vegetarian hard cheese, optional
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the pasta until just tender, then reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  3. Warm olive oil in a large pan and cook the garlic for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the cream, then add the drained pasta.
  6. Loosen the sauce with reserved pasta water as needed.
  7. Finish with basil and optional cheese before serving.
